## 📌 Project Overview
This VAT Compliance Tracker was developed using a dataset sourced from Kaggle. The data was cleaned and transformed using Power Query, including the conversion and formatting of monetary values into Nigerian Naira (₦).
The dashboard is designed to monitor VAT compliance across customers, track VAT attributable to individual customers, and provide actionable insights through interactive Excel visualizations and reporting. The analysis includes total sales and their corresponding VAT obligations, customer-level VAT performance, compliance status tracking, and the percentage of filing periods completed before their due dates. These insights help users evaluate VAT compliance trends, identify potential risks, and support informed decision-making.
## 🔄 Data Cleaning & Transformation
The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query to ensure it was suitable for analysis. Duplicate records and inconsistencies were removed, missing values were handled appropriately, and date and numerical fields were standardized to ensure accuracy and consistency across the dataset. The data was also converted from USD to Nigerian Naira (₦), and a pseudo column was created to support further analysis and enhance the dataset structure.

## ⏱️ Filing Timeliness Analysis
The analysis included evaluating the percentage of VAT filings submitted early and those submitted late in relation to their due dates. This helped measure overall compliance efficiency and identify filing behavior patterns across customers.Excel functions such as `IF`, `COUNTIF`, and `COUNTA` were used to classify and calculate the proportions of early and late submissions accurately.

## 📊 Product Category Performance
The analysis revealed that the **Book category** recorded the highest sales and the highest VAT remitted compared to all other product categories. This highlights the strong performance of books in driving overall revenue and VAT contribution within the dataset, making it the most significant category in terms of financial impact.

## 📌 Conclusion
The analysis revealed that 93% of VAT filings were submitted early, while 7% were submitted late, indicating a strong overall level of compliance across customers. A pseudo column was also created to enhance the dataset structure and support more effective analysis.
Overall, the core outcome of this project is the VAT Compliance Tracker, which provides a clear view of each customer’s compliance status, VAT-attributable sales, and total VAT obligations. The tracker serves as a comprehensive tool for monitoring compliance performance and supporting informed decision-making.
